Becoming a Member:


BAPTISM:

For persons who have become Christ-Followers by receiving Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior and desire to obey Christ by being baptized by immersion. Contact Pastor Shane Brewer if you have any questions about baptism.



STATEMENT OF FAITH:

For persons who are saved, have been baptized by immersion after salvation, and are members in good standing of another Baptist church that will provide a letter of recommendation to Fort Mitchell Baptist Church. Our Deacons!

The Deacon Body meets monthly on the third Tuesday at 6:00 p.m., in the Conference Room of Fort Mitchell Baptist Church. Every family who attends FMBC is assigned a family deacon, who communicates with the families on a regular basis. If there is a crisis in the family, the deacon is on call to assist. During the monthly meetings the deacons spend time in leadership training to learn how to be a better disciple for Christ. Deacon terms are for three years, and the selection of Deacons occurs on an annual basis as Deacons rotate off.
